Cowen discussing his book *Talent* (co-authored with Daniel Gross). The practical interview advice is useful and the framework for thinking about what raw talent looks like is interesting. Less rigorous than his academic work but that's partly the point — these are heuristics, not models.

The conversation about how unconventional questions reveal things standard interviews miss is the most memorable part.
