"Piranesi" is a novel by Clarke, falling under the genre of Mythology. The reader started reading the book on April 24, 2023, and the current status is ongoing.

The novel revolves around the character of Piranesi, who is the only inhabitant of a mysterious house with endless rooms and corridors. He spends his days exploring the house and recording his observations in his journal. However, his solitary existence is disrupted when he meets a man named "The Other", who claims to share his interest in the secrets of the house.

As Piranesi and The Other delve deeper into the mysteries of the house, they discover dark secrets that challenge their understanding of reality. The novel is a complex exploration of identity, memory, and the power of human imagination.

Overall, "Piranesi" is a thought-provoking and engaging novel that offers a unique perspective on the world of mythology.
